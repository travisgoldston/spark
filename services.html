What we do, in plain English.

You don’t need everything at once. We help you choose the right mix. Here’s what each service delivers.

Be the business people actually find.

What we do

We make sure your website and Google listing tell Google what you do, where you serve, and who you’re for, so your site can rank properly when people search nearby.

What you get

  • More calls from people who say “I found you on Google”
  • Form fills from areas you actually want
  • Fewer empty days on the calendar

Turn search into calls, without wasting budget.

What we do

We run Google Ads so you show up when people are already searching for what you offer. We trim what doesn’t work and focus on what does.

What you get

  • Calls within days or weeks from people ready to book
  • Clear sense of which services bring the best leads
  • Control: we scale up or pause based on results

Web design that turns visitors into calls.

What we do

We design (or redesign) a clear, fast site that makes it obvious what you do, where you serve, and how to reach you. Mobile first, simple, and built to earn trust quickly.

What you get

  • More people clicking “Call” or “Request a quote”
  • A site that feels modern, trustworthy, and easy to use
Why it all fits together

Visibility works best when the pieces line up.

SEO gets you found in organic search and on the map. Google Ads gets you in front of people who are searching right now. Your website turns that click into trust so they call or fill out a form instead of bouncing. We don’t push you into all three at once. We help you choose what makes sense for your budget and goals. Many businesses start with one (often fixing the basics and SEO), then add ads or a site refresh when it’s right. Our How it works page walks through the process, and our Pricing page gives you a sense of what to expect.

Local first

Most of our clients serve a local area, plumbers, dentists, contractors, cleaners, therapists. If your customers search “your service near me,” we make sure you show up clearly and accurately.

Plain language

We don’t hand you dashboards or jargon. You get short updates in plain English: what we did, what we’re seeing, and what we’re doing next. You stay in the loop without learning marketing.

One contact

You work with us directly, no account managers, no handoffs. You can text or call when you have a question. We’re a small team (husband and wife) who want to see small businesses succeed. More on our About us page.

What to expect

We do the work. You get updates.

You’re not handed a list of tasks or a dashboard to learn. We handle the technical side, optimizing your listing, writing or updating pages, setting up and monitoring ads, and we report back in plain English. You’ll know what we did, what we’re seeing, and what we’re doing next. If something isn’t working, we’ll say so and adjust. Our goal is steady, predictable lead flow so you can focus on running your business. If you’re curious about the step by step process, see How it works.
