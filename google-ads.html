What it is

Google Ads puts you in front of people searching right now

Ads show your business when someone searches for a service like yours. You pay when they click. The goal is simple: spend money, get leads, and keep the waste low.

  • Best for: When you need leads soon, or you are in a competitive market.
  • Not a fit: If you cannot answer calls, follow up, or handle more work.
  • Good pairing: Ads plus SEO and a clear website.

How we run Ads

We keep it simple and measurable

Most wasted ad spend comes from broad targeting, poor landing pages, and no ongoing cleanup. We focus on the basics that actually move results.

  1. Start focused: We pick the services that matter most and the areas you actually want.
  2. Track real leads: Calls and forms matter. Clicks alone do not.
  3. Cut waste weekly: We block bad searches, improve ads, and adjust bids so you pay for fewer wrong clicks.
