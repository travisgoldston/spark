What it is

SEO is how you earn clicks without paying for each one.

When someone searches on Google, they see a map and a list of businesses. SEO is the work that helps you show up there, and helps your site make sense to Google.

  • Local SEO: Your Google Business Profile and how you show up in map results.
  • Website SEO: Your pages, services, and location signals so Google knows what you do and where you serve.
  • Trust signals: Reviews, consistent info, and a site that looks real and easy to contact.

No dashboard homework. We do the work and explain it in plain English.
