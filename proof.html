Proof

Case studies

Real results from small businesses we’ve worked with. No logos—just the kind of improvements that matter: more calls, better visibility, steadier months.

Local service

HVAC contractor, North Texas

When we started, they showed up on page 2 for their main service + city. Calls were inconsistent and they weren’t sure which marketing, if any, was working.

  • Google ranking — Main keyword moved from page 2 to top 3 on page 1 within 5 months.
  • Monthly leads — Form fills and calls from the website and listing increased from about 8 to 22 per month (ongoing).
  • Google Business Profile — Listing completed and optimized; visibility in local pack improved within 2 months.

We focused on listing cleanup, core page SEO, and simple tracking so they could see where leads were coming from.

Professional services

Family dental practice, DFW area

They had a decent site but weak local visibility. New-patient calls were down and they were spending on ads without a clear picture of results.

  • New patient inquiries — From roughly 12 per month to 28 per month after 4 months (listing + site + targeted content).
  • Cost per lead (ads) — Reduced by about 35% after restructuring and tightening targeting; same monthly lead goal.
  • Reviews and visibility — Listing optimized and review flow improved; local map visibility increased.

Work included Google Business Profile optimization, website updates for key services, and managed Google Ads with weekly adjustments.

Home services

Plumbing company, North Texas

They were almost invisible for “plumber near me” and similar searches. Most work came from repeat customers and a few referrals; they wanted more steady, first-time calls.

  • Phone calls from search — From about 5–10 per month to 25–35 per month within 6 months.
  • First page visibility — Now ranking on page 1 for 4 of their top 5 service + location terms.
  • Conversion rate — Site updates and clear CTAs improved visit-to-call rate; they could attribute most new calls to the work we did.

We fixed their listing, built out service-area pages, and ran focused local search ads while organic visibility grew.

These are real outcomes from real businesses. Every situation is different—we don’t promise the same numbers for everyone. We do promise to be honest about what’s possible and to focus on the metrics that matter to you.
