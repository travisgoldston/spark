Our approach

How we work and what you can expect.

We don’t have case studies to show yet. We’re new. What we can show is our process: how we’ll work with you, what we’ll report, and how we measure success so you’re never in the dark.

Process

Audit → Optimize → Grow.

Same three steps we describe on How it works. We start by seeing where you stand, fix the basics so you can rank and get found, then keep improving so leads stay steady. No surprises, no black box.

  • Audit: We look at your Google listing, your site, and where you show up (or don’t). You tell us your goals.
  • Optimize: We fix your listing, your pages, and your content so Google can rank you properly and people can find you.
  • Grow: We monitor, adjust, and report so you get a steady stream of calls and form fills.
Transparency

You’ll know what we did and what we’re seeing.

We don’t hand you a dashboard full of jargon. You get short, plain English updates: what we did this month, what’s improving, and what we’re doing next. If something isn’t working, we’ll say so and adjust. We’d rather be honest than impressive.

  • Monthly reports in plain English
  • You can text or call us directly, no ticket system
  • Ad spend is separate and in your control
What we optimize for

Metrics that matter to you.

We focus on outcomes you care about: more calls, more form fills, better visibility for the searches that bring you customers. We don’t chase vanity numbers. We’ll agree with you up front what “good” looks like, for example, a certain number of leads per month, or showing up on page one for key terms, and we’ll report against that.
