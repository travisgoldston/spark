What’s really going on online

If you’re not clearly marked, you don’t exist.

Google is the map

When someone searches “your service near me”, Google shows them a map and a list. If you’re not on that map, or you’re buried under others, most people never even see your name.

Your marker is fuzzy

Maybe your address is wrong. Maybe your listing is half-filled. Maybe Google isn’t sure what you actually do. When things are unclear, the system plays it safe and shows another business instead.

Most marketing is set-and-forget

A lot of marketing work happens once and then gets ignored. Pages don’t get updated. Ads don’t get tuned. Listings drift out of date. Over time, your visibility fades and the phone slows down again.

Spark helps you get clearly placed on the map — and stay there.
