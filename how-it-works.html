A calm, clear plan

How it works

Three steps: Audit, Optimize, Grow. No dashboards. No big meetings. Built for busy owners who want more calls, not more homework.

The process

Audit → Optimize → Grow

You stay focused on your business. We handle getting you found online and report back in plain English.

  1. We look at how you show up on Google and on your website right now. You tell us what you do, where you serve, and what “good” looks like. More calls, a steadier calendar. One short call (10 to 15 minutes). No sales pitch. We’re working from facts so the next steps make sense.

  2. We fix the basics so people can actually find you: your Google listing accurate and complete, your main pages clear about what you do and where, your contact details easy to tap and call. We make you look as good online as you are in person. Real photos, clear services, so strangers feel safe choosing you. Depending on your goals, we may add search improvements, local visibility, and conversion improvements so more visitors become leads.

  3. We keep an eye on what’s working and what’s not. Quick check ins by text or call. We adjust instead of letting things slide. The goal: a steady stream of people ready to book, so you can focus on running your business.

Your time

Minimal time from you.

  • At the start: One focused call and a few emails or texts to gather details.
  • Ongoing: Most owners spend less than an hour a month. Quick check ins, plain English updates. No dashboards to learn.

What we actually do in that time: Services ( SEO, Web Design, Conversion Optimization ). What it costs: Pricing.

Why this feels different

You get us, not an account manager.

We’re a small team. When you work with us, you work with the people doing the work. You have our contact; you can text or call when something’s off or you want a quick update. No ticket system, no week long wait for a reply. More on About us.
